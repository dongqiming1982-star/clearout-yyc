# Calgary pricing notes used in the estimator

This version calibrates the public-facing estimator against public Calgary market references gathered on 2026-05-04:

- Minimum / single-item pickup: roughly C$100-C$150
- Mattress pickup: around C$129
- Sofa pickup: around C$148
- Quarter truck load: roughly C$150-C$250
- Half truck load: roughly C$250-C$385
- Full truck load: roughly C$400-C$650
- City disposal baseline: City of Calgary landfill/Eco Centre minimum is C$25 for charge items under 250 kg; commercial basic sanitary waste rate is C$113/tonne.

These numbers are estimates only. Final provider quotes may change based on volume, weight, access, parking, stairs/elevator, timing, landfill/disposal fees, and on-site confirmation.
